Article

Life, consisting of both matter and energy, is a precious gift bestowed upon all of us. To demonstrate our appreciation for life, we should actively participate in the myriad of celebrations that surround us during our time on Earth. Every moment we experience, whether it's marked by happiness, sorrow, victory, or defeat, brings us one step closer to the culmination of our existence. As humans, we often neglect to contemplate the circle of life. Instead, we fixate on our pursuit of pleasure and happiness, yearning for an everlasting life. Why do we fail to comprehend the straightforward wisdom of nature, which is to find happiness in every moment, making that very moment a celebration of being alive? Life is not solely a sequence of experiences, milestones, events, happenings, or accomplishments; it is also a collection of moments. However, we mustn't forget that we are all born with a finite amount of time.

Ultimately, the goal of life is to reconnect with nature. (Edited. Source : CRAM.com).
